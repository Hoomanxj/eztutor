Join us as we delve into the fascinating stories of these famous monkeys and their incredible contributions to our world!

Koko - The Talking Gorilla

Species: Western Lowland Gorilla
Koko was famous for her ability to communicate using American Sign Language. She formed deep bonds with her trainers and showed the world that gorillas could have complex emotions, intelligence, and even a sense of humor.

Congo - The Painting Ape

Species: Chimpanzee
Congo became famous in the 1950s for his abstract paintings, which even caught the attention of Pablo Picasso! He was known for his distinct style and consistency, and his artwork was exhibited in galleries and sold at auctions.

Albert II - The First Primate in Space

Species: Rhesus Monkey
Albert II was the first monkey to successfully survive a spaceflight, although he tragically died upon re-entry due to a parachute failure. His mission in 1949 marked a significant milestone in the early stages of space exploration.
